Overview

Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) are 2 competing neural networks that are able to generate specific content based on a given dataset.

I used them to generate fake celebrities and cats but their different variants can be applied to solve some complex problems.

Some examples would be generating molecular structures to be tested in the early parts of the drug discovery process, generating the new virtual landscapes for video games and movies or even creating art and music.
